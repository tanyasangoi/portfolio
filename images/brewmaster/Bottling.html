Bottling & Carbonation

At the end of fermentation, we now have alcoholic beer, but it is still flat and uncarbonated.

This beer is then bottled, as shown on the left.

While being bottled, it is carbonated, as shown on the right. It is either artificially carbonated like a soda, or ‘bottle conditioned’, where it’s allowed to naturally carbonate via the carbon dioxide the yeast produces.

After allowing it to age for anywhere from a few weeks to a few months, the beer is ready for the final step!
