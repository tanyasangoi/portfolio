Hello There

Get to know me better

I am a graduate student at the University of Washington, studying User Experience Design & Information Architecture.

The words of Pierre Reverdy, "Creativity is to think more efficiently" echo my design philosophy and I believe that great design should be clean, effortless and colorful.

I want to live by cliches like “Cover the earth before the earth covers you”. When I am not busy brainstorming my next idea or planning my next trip, you can find me writing poetry, coloring my hair red and discovering new music.
