Meet my team!
I had the chance to work with some talented designers having a diverse skill set ranging from graphic design to interaction design.

We had regular stand-ups and design reviews to discuss the progress of our projects and provide creative feedback for each other's work.

This is me creating an affinity map of some user testing results.
I helped my team in different stages of the design process along with research and testing.
My projects involved redesigning various aspects of the e-commerce website and the Samsung Support Pages.

For a newly assembled team, breaking the ice was a crucial first step to becoming an efficient and effective design team.
As an intern, I took the initiative to organize various team-building events such as the UX Social which helped us get to know each other better.
